Account Services

A complete bouquet of APIs for authenticated customers to have access to information pertaining to their accounts and transactions. The offering provides robust and flexibility access to information for customers from everything ranging from - account balance, mini-statements, transaction status or raising service requests. This is the one-stop shop to provide you with a plethora of accessibility to one's banking portfolio. If you are a fintech or aggregator who wants to integrate with our services to provide world-class user experience for your users based on the authentication provided by customers, do check out our offerings.

Services Description
Fixed Deposit A set of APIs to support Fixed Deposit lifecycle, enabling innovative solutions to support end to end customer journey, from initiating the customer request for FD, establishing customer relationship , enabling FD process for creation and management through statements & services.
Recurring Deposit A set of APIs to support Recurring Deposit lifecycle, enabling innovative solutions to support end to end customer journey, from initiating the customer request for RD, establishing customer relationship , enabling RD process for creation and management through statements & services.
Current Accounts A set of APIs to support Current Account lifecycle, enabling innovative solutions to support end to end customer journey, from initiating the customer request for Current Account, establishing customer relationship , enabling Current Account process for creation and management through statements & services.
