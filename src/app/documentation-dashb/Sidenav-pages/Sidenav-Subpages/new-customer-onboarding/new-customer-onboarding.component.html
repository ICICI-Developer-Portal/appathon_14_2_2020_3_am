New Customer Onboarding

In day-to-day working, most of the times Businesses need to open bank accounts for either their customers or their employees, these APIs allow you to open a bank account for an individual without making them to visit any branch. With various modular products you can decide what facilities you want to provide to your customer, ranging from Face Scan APIs to Credit Card Ordering APIs.

Services Description
Demographic Details This API will give demographic features.
Account This API service is used to choose account number of users choice and blocks account number if it is available
Additional Features This API will give additional features.
