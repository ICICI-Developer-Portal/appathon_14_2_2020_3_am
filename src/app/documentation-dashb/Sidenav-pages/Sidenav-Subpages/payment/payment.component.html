Payments

Whatever be the nature of payments UPI, NEFT or IMPS or any other. ICICI Bank developer portal provides you with complete visibility across-the-board with our APIs whether for individual customers or merchants. The bouquet of APIs allow you to cover the complete lifecycle from registration to making transactions, checking status and de-registering. Merchants and organizations that need to place bulk remittance transactions and track their status, we provide solutions to ease the pain. These robust APIs facilitate you to traverse across this process with ease whether it is- placing requests, facilitating payments, tracking requests or publishing consolidations. So whether its an organization, institution or cooperative society, these API services facilitate easy creation of the needful for bulk remittances and their tracking.

Services Description
EazyPay 'Eazypay is a first of its kind secure payment service by ICICI Bank in India. It enables institutions to collect money from their customers through multiple payment modes. The APIs allow merchants to have a complete transactional flow ranging from QR code generation for the merchant, send collect request, check transaction status, and claim for refund.'
